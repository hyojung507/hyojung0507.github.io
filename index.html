Designed a typographic poster promoting RISD Museum's
exhibits and events in 2015. Through the white dots,
spatial atmosphere of the poster is created; by the placement
of behind and over the type, the emphasis of "space" is being made.
Second version of RISD Museum's exhibition poster.
A hypothetical lecture poster for four sculptors:
Do Ho Suh, Richard Tuttle, Sol LeWitt, and Agnes Martin.
All four of their works carry a similar theme of "absence".
By the spatial atmosphere created by transparent laser-cut
acrylic letters and overall texture, absence was formulated.
The missing 'E' in the acrylic letters is created through
the negative space formed by the text blocks.
