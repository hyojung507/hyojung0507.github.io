deCordova Rebrand

Rebrand of deCordova Sculpture Park and Museum in Boston. The deCordova is a public space consisting both inside and outside - of sculptures and various art pieces. Providing a constantly changing landscape of large-scale, outdoor, modern and contemporary sculpture and site-specific installations, the Sculpture Park hosts more than 60 works, the majority which are on loan to the Museum. Inside, the Museum features a robust slate of rotating exhibitions. Thus, focusing on the changing aspect and the simplicity of the contemporary art, the rebrand emphasizes on a single line: simple and flexible.
