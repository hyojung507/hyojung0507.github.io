Time and Relativity Association "TARA"

Exercise for a prompt: "The government is developing a new consumer time travel program to be called TARA (Time And Relativity Association), which needs to speak to the civilian audience. Develop an identity system for TARA that establishes its brand. Prepare a guideline that demonstrates how this brand can be universally applied, including some environmental examples such as a logo in a cockpit, or on a ray gun. Make sure that proper use, and misuse examples are clearly documented."

The TARA app is a tool for time travel users both first time and frequent users. The app consists of different parts, such as reservation (booking a trip) and reviews from past travelers for certain time travel locations.

01. Reservation
This certain app interaction shows the narrative of a user booking a trip. User may first choose which continent to go, then the next page shows the list of countries in the continent with pictures.

02. Reservation
On the bottom is where the user can choose the date to travel either to the past or future, by choosing the year. When user selects a country, it goes to details page, where the user may actually reserve and book the trip — with specifics. There is also reviews that past travelers wrote.
