Absence

A hypothetical lecture poster for four sculptors: Do Ho Suh, Richard Tuttle, Sol LeWitt, and Agnes Martin. All four of their works carry a similar theme of "absence". By the spatial atmosphere created by transparent laser-cut acrylic letters and overall texture, absence was formulated. The missing 'E' in the acrylic letters is created through the negative space formed by the text blocks.
